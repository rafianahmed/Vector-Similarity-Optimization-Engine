What changed

This version reduces wrong rankings by combining semantic labels from MobileNet, deep embedding similarity, color histogram similarity, and a grayscale mismatch penalty. It also uses a stronger Bayesian-inspired calibration layer to optimize weights, threshold, and probability shift on a pseudo-labeled validation split.

That means a cat image is less likely to be outranked by a grayscale human image just because of texture overlap.
